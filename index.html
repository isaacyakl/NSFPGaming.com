about

Nah Son, Free Pie! is a close-knit gaming community built around the idea of playing what you love, with people you like.

We have a presence in Minecraft, Rocket League, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Apex Legends, Battlefield, and whatever else anyone happens to be playing (we love to try out new games). We are of various ages and skill levels. We are primarily social gamers with the goal of having fun and building lasting friendships.

Oh, and in case you're wondering, we took our inspiration from our pie enjoying friend!
