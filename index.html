about

Nah Son, Free Pie! is a close-knit gaming community for people built around the idea of playing what you love, with people you like.

We have a presence in Minecraft, GTA online, Rocket League, Counter-Strike:Global Offensive, Battlefield, and whatever else anyone happens to be playing. We offer servers in a few different games, depending on what is currently hot as well as maintaining a Teamspeak server where members meet regularly for nights of fun! If you are interested in join us, head on over to the Steam or Facebook group and get in contact with an admin.

Oh, and in case you're wondering, we took our inspiration from our pie enjoying friend!
